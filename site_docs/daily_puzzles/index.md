# Daily Puzzles

One new biology puzzle every day, Wordle-style. Each puzzle rotates at
local midnight, tracks your guess streak in your browser (no account
needed), and is designed to take two to five minutes. Come back
tomorrow for a fresh challenge.

## How it works

- A fresh puzzle unlocks at local midnight each day.
- Your streak, last-played date, and guess history are stored in your
  browser's local storage, so clearing site data will reset your
  stats.
- Each puzzle has a built-in help panel. If the game area looks empty,
  open the "How to solve" section on that puzzle's page.
- Puzzles are open-source teaching tools; see the
  [Author](../author.md) page for credits and the
  [License](../license.md) for reuse terms.
